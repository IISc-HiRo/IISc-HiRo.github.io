Prospective Students

We are always looking for motivated students interested in robotics, control theory, machine learning, and human-robot interaction. View our research at Google Scholar. If you are interested in joining our lab:

  • PhD applicants: Apply through the IISc admissions portal.
  • Master's students: If you want to pursue your master's project, visit Prof. Ravi Prakash directly at his office.
  • Project Associates and UG interns: Please email Prof. Ravi Prakash with your interests and CV.

Collaborations

Interested in collaboration? Please reach out to Prof. Ravi Prakash at ravipr@iisc.ac.in.
